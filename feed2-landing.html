How it works
Three steps.
One less excuse.

Feed2 works quietly in the background, turning passive saves into an active second memory.

1
Capture from any feed
Save anything — a tweet, a link, a title mentioned in passing. Feed2 catches it all, regardless of platform.
2
Structure automatically
AI identifies what you saved — book, film, article, idea — and builds a clean, searchable card with context and key quotes.
3
Get reminded at the right time
Feed2 surfaces your saved items when you're likely to act on them. Not immediately. Not never. At the right moment.
